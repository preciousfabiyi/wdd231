Section 01

Site Name

Fife Beauty Hub & Spa

The name Fife Beauty Hub & Spa was chosen because it combines the owner's personal brand identity ("Fife") with a clear description of the services offered — beauty treatments and spa experiences. The word "Hub" communicates that the site is a central destination for all beauty-related needs, while "Spa" signals a premium, wellness-focused environment.

The name is short, memorable, and professional — ideal for both word-of-mouth referrals and online search visibility.

fifebeautyhub.com
Section 02

Site Purpose

The Fife Beauty Hub & Spa website serves as a full-service online presence for a professional beauty and wellness business based in Ilorin, Nigeria.

The site provides customers with the ability to:

  • Browse and purchase curated beauty and skincare products
  • Book appointments for services such as facials, luxury pedicures, and skin consultations
  • Learn about available services and pricing
  • Submit payment confirmation screenshots for online orders
  • Leave reviews and read testimonials from other clients
  • Contact the business via phone, email, and social media

The site also includes a secure admin portal where the business owner can upload and manage products, view customer orders, confirm or reject payments, and monitor business activity — all from any device.

Section 03

Scenarios

The following questions represent the kinds of things a target visitor — a beauty-conscious customer in Nigeria — would want answered when visiting the site:

  • "What skincare products do you carry, and how much do they cost?"
  • "How do I book a facial or pedicure appointment, and what times are available?"
  • "I just made a bank transfer — how do I send proof of payment for my order?"
  • "What do other customers say about the spa experience here?"
  • "Where is the spa located, and how can I reach you by phone or WhatsApp?"

Section 05

Typography

Two complementary typefaces are used throughout the site. The pairing balances editorial elegance with clean modern readability.

Font 1 Headings · Section titles · Logo text · Display

Cormorant Garamond

Glow Naturally, With Confidence

A refined serif typeface with elegant proportions. Used for all headings (h1–h3), section titles, the logo wordmark, and large display text. Weights used: 400, 600, 700. Also used in italic for decorative emphasis.

Font 2 Body text · Navigation · Buttons · Labels · UI

DM Sans — Clear, Modern, Readable

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. Used for all paragraph text, navigation links, button labels, form inputs, and UI elements throughout the site.

A geometric sans-serif designed for high legibility on screens at all sizes. Weights used: 300 (light body), 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semi-bold buttons).
